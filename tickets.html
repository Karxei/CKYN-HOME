Tickets and Packages

Family Package

For 2 adults and 1-3 children under the age of 12. For the children we offer free access to every attraction and 10 % discount in the CKYN restaurant.

Friends Package

A group ticket for 4-8 persons. 15 % discount in the CKYN restaurant.

Not Alone Ticket

An option for an exciting date! Free dessert in the CKYN dessert shop.

Blue Package

The holder of the Blue Package has the possibility to share access with 3 other people and will get 20% discount in the CKYN restaurant. Valid for 5 entries for 4 months.

The Rock

Single ticket with free entrance to the aquarium.

Monday-Tuesday = 12 pm to 7 pm

Wednesday-Thursday = 10 am to 7 pm
