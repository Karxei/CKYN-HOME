Event Tickets and Packages

!Tickets and Packages price changes according to the events which are gonna held except Red Smoke Package!

The Rock

A ticket for one person, and you have to buy this ticket with every Package and any type of ticket listed below.

Family Package

A family which includes two adults and 1 to 2 childern under 10 years old= Free entraces for the childern in any events and 35 % discount for food.

Friends Package

A group of people(3 to 5)= 2 free tickets for any events and 20% discount tickets for others 3 people to any events.

Not alone Ticket

A couple= 50 % discount for the Rock ticket and free ticket for one person. Will also get a couple dessert at CKYN dessert shop.

Red Smoke

A package for 4 months= if somenone own this package, the person can bring 3 people to the park and will get 40 % discount for every services in the theme park for 1 person and will get 50% discount for any tickets to an event. ( This package can be used 5 times within 4 months.)

ColorLy

A Package for one person to any events and 40 % discount for food.
